30/******************************************************************************
31* This module is based on the iterative algorithm of [S.Y.Hu, L.Q.Chen, *
32* Acta. Mater. 49 (2001) 1879 - 1890] with modifications allowing to use *
33* inhomogeneous elasticity parameters not limited to composition dependence. *
34* The boundary condition allowing free volume expansion is also introduced *
35* following the approach outlined in [I.Steinbach, M.Apel, Physica D 217 *
36* (2006) 153 - 160]. *
37* Finite strain extension considers Green-Lagrangian strain within the *
38* St. Venant-Kirchhoff hyper-elastic model. The resulting solver algorithm *
39* is similar to [P. Eisenlohr et al., International Journal of Plasticity 46 *
40* (2013) 37â€“53] *
41******************************************************************************/
